# [Marion Tavares]()

dedicated "Fighter" for the group, even though Marion naturally works well as a Flex character.

Deck Theme: The deck is heavily built around playing a massive number of events, humorously named "probably plays a bunch of events".

Key Assets/Non-Events:

He is running "Meat Hook" and "Beat Cop" to boost his combat stat.

He relies on the new "Remington" firearm, which he treats essentially as an event because of how well it combos with cards like "Act of Desperation" and "Push to the Limit".

He includes "Hallowed Mirror," which is an asset that effectively adds three powerful events to his deck.

Skills:

"Take the Initiative" is used for protection, compensating for Marion's low willpower (brain) stat of 2.

"Vicious Blow" is used for extra damage output.

Events Strategy:

Economy & Utility: He uses "Clean Them Out" and "Emergency Cache" to fund his plays.

Clues & Movement: "Task Force" is highly valued because it synergizes with the Remington and allows him to chain into other events, while "Look what I found!" is used to fail an investigation safely and grab clues.

Combat: For dealing damage, he relies on "Toe to Toe" and "One-Two Punch".

Survivor Cards: He splashes Survivor cards like "End of the Road" and "Improvised Weapon" to benefit from fast events and graveyard recursion.

Upgrade Plans:

He plans to swap out "Vicious Blow" for "Hunter's Mark".

He specifically aims to upgrade to the Level 5 version of "One-Two Punch" as soon as he can.

Other immediate upgrade targets include "Quick Shot," the upgraded "Remington," and "The True Awakening" (a Specialist card).

He also acknowledges a current lack of card draw and plans to heavily invest in card draw upgrades to become "unstoppable".
